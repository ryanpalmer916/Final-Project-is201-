About the Giants

The San Francisco Giants are an American professional baseball team based in San Francisco. The Giants compete in Major League Baseball (MLB) as a member club of the National League (NL) West division. Founded in 1883 as the New York Gothams, and renamed three years later the New York Giants, the team eventually moved to San Francisco in 1958.

The franchise is one of the oldest and most successful in professional baseball, with more wins than any team in the history of major American sports. The team was the first major league team based in New York City, most memorably playing home games at several iterations of the Polo Grounds before moving to San Francisco.

Team History

The Giants have won eight World Series championships and 23 National League pennants. The franchise has also won 107 games in the 2021 season, setting a franchise record for wins.
